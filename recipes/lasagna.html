Lasagna


Image credit: Yatha Pradipta (Flickr)

Ingredients

  • 1 (or more) Costco Frozen Lasagna

Preparation

  1. Go to Costco.
  2. Buy frozen Lasagna.
  3. Bring lasagna home.
  4. Put lasagna in oven.
  5. Cook until done.
  6. Serve with a sprig of parsley to make it look fancy.
  7. Enjoy.
